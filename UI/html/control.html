Control panel

This panel will allow you to interact with the DHT. Before that, you need to follow these two steps :

  • first, be sure to have connected your browser by entering your GMail or Jabber credentials above, and clicking the Connect button
  • then make sure to join the network by clicking on the Join button. This will start an iterative lookup to search for nodes close to you

After that, feel free to post and retrieve data from the DHT and to take a look at all the different panels to see what is happening behind the scene.
